How to Play:

Get a password by subscribing to one of the OnlyFans accounts listed on the Home page.

Click one of the Bet buttons depending on how much you wish to stake. Both players start with $400.

You will be dealt 2 cards, and the stripper will be dealt 2 cards, with one visible to you.

If you get blackjack (total of 21), you will win 1.5 times your stake.

Cards are worth their face value. J, Q, K, are worth 10, and A is worth 11 or 1.

The goal is to get your hand as close to 21 as possible without going over.

Click hit to add another card to your hand.

If your hand goes over 21, you bust and lose your stake.

Click stand to end your turn

The stripper will then reveal her hidden card, and hit or stand.

If the stripper busts (goes over 21), you win her stake.

Whoever has the highest total otherwise wins this round.

If there is a tie, all bets are returned.

Each time you win $100, the stripper will take off one article of clothing!

Have fun!
